Core features

ValidTill focuses on the practical details people actually need when managing expiring coupons and stored-value cards.

Flexible coupon records

Save value, expiry date, notes, links, card numbers, sender information, and a starred or used state for each item.

Expiry reminders

Schedule local notifications before upcoming expiry dates so time-sensitive savings stay visible when they matter.

Widget support

Keep key coupon information on the Home Screen with widgets that reflect your latest local data.

What to expect

The current app experience is intentionally lightweight and private by default.

  • No account creation or sign-in is required.
  • Coupon data is stored locally on device.
  • English and Simplified Chinese are supported in the app.
  • Notifications are local notifications, not remote push messages.
  • Optional avatar selection uses the system photo picker when you choose a photo.
